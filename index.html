About Our Club

Welcome to the Coding Club! We are a community of passionate developers, coders, and tech enthusiasts dedicated to building innovative projects, improving coding skills, and helping each other grow. By joining the Error Club, you agree to the following terms:

Membership

Membership is open to individuals interested in the club’s mission. Applications must be truthful. The club can approve or deny applications.

Code of Conduct

All members must be respectful and professional. Harassment, discrimination, or misconduct will result in removal.

Participation

Active involvement in club activities and timely task completion are expected. Repeated absences without notice may lead to termination.

Intellectual Property

Projects developed under the club belong to the club unless specified otherwise. Unauthorized use or sharing of projects is prohibited.

Resources

Club resources are for official activities only. Misuse may lead to penalties.

Privacy

Members' personal information will remain confidential. Do not disclose club-related information without approval.

Event Participation

Members must represent the club professionally in external events and follow its mission and values.

Termination

The club may terminate memberships for violations. Members may withdraw voluntarily by providing notice.

Updates

The club may update these terms anytime. Members will be informed of changes, and continued participation means acceptance.

Limitation of Liability

The club is not responsible for any personal loss, injury, or damage during participation. Members join at their own risk.
